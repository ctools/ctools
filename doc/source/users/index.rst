User Documentation
==================

This documentation will guide you in the process of using ctools. Some of the
manual pages and tutorials are Jupyter Notebooks that you can recognize by the
Jupyter logo at the beginning of the page.

.. image:: ../jupyter-logo.svg

To download the notebooks and execute them on
your machine go to the page and right click on "Show Source" at the bottom of
the right navigation bar.
